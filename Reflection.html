Reflection

Working on this project, I have gained a lot of insights into graphic design, web design, and typography. Graphic design has been a challenging area for me, and I felt like I could only achieve the basic requirement to use four principles in my work. The outcome was not good at all. Viewing various poster designs online inspired me to design better. During a discussion with my professor, she provided me with guidance like I could try to change the direction of the text. So it would not be like just randomly putting the information in the corner. The advice improved the visual impact of my poster. Despite my work not meeting my personal expectations, I was proud of the effort and dedication I had put into this learning process.


For my website, I designed an ocean theme. I added some marine life illustrations for visual appeal. Initially, as I completed the coursework so quickly, the professor provided me with some feedback about the traditional font style used in the title. I could seek a more suitable option. As I recall I had learned to change some style in coding worksheet 5, so I chose something cute in Google Fonts and applied it to my website. As the background was too monotony. Chandra (2023) provided some examples of background patterns, so I learned from them and decorated my website.


One of the challenges I encountered while constructing my website was implementing responsive design, ensuring that the content restructures gracefully when the browser window is resized.
